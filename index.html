About Us

Our Story

Welcome to Hideout Café, a student-friendly establishment serving quick and tasty meals since 2025. We take pride in offering a delightful mix of Indian and Asian fast foods, beverages, and quick bites that cater to all tastes and preferences.

Our café provides a cozy atmosphere where you can escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life and enjoy delicious food with friends and family.

Run by Chef Thapa

Our talented Chef Thapa brings years of culinary expertise to create the perfect blend of flavors that will tantalize your taste buds.
